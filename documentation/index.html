Commands

Movement by character

h Left
j Down
k Up
l Right
spacebar Right
BACKSPACE Left
CTRL-H Left

Movement by text

w Forward by 'word'
b Backward by 'word'
W Forward by 'WORD'
B Backward by 'WORD'
e End of word
E End of WORD
ge End of previous WORD
gE End of previous WORD
) Beginning of next sentence
( Beginning of current sentence
} Beginning of next paragraph
{ Beginning of current paragraph
]] Beginning of next section
[[ Beginning of current section
][ End of next section
[ ] End of current section

Movement by lines

0 First position of current line
$ Last position of current line
^ First nonblank character in current line
_ Last nonblank character of current line
+ First nonblank chracter of next line
- First nonblank character of previous line
ENTER First nonblank character of next line
_num_ | Column _num_ of current line
g0 First position of screen line
g$ Last position of screen line
g^ First nonblank character of screen line
gm Middle of screen line
gk Move up one screen line
gj Move down one screen line
H Top line of screen
M Middle line of screen
L Last line of screen
_num_ H _num_ lines after top line
_num_ L _num lines before last line
